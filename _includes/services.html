Usage

Typical Uses for Purchasing Toolpak

Strategic Sourcing

Develop sourcing strategies, identify key suppliers, complete multi-round bid events and indentify strategic suppliers to meet business needs.

Procure to Pay (P2P)

Improve buyer transactional efficiency and team productivity. Spend more time on strategic sourcing activities.

Supplier Integration and Risk Management

Work with suppliers to develop and implement continuous improvement plans to drive quality, innovation and cost. Identify high risk suppliers and develop effective contingency plans.
